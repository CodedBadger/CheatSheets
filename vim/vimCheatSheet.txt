### MOVEMENT ###
 - h=left | j=down | k=up | l=right
 - w=jump to right of next word | b=jump to left of next word
 - ^ beginning of line | $=last character of line
 - gg=first line | G=last line | nG=(n=lineNumber) Go to line
 - ctrl+d= down 1/2 page | ctrl+u= up 1/2 page
 - ctrl+b= back full page | ctrl+f=foward full page
